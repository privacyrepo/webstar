Managing your subscription

No lock-in, no hoops. Here's exactly how billing works and how to cancel whenever you want.

Your 5-day free trial

When you sign up, your card isn't charged for 5 days. Cancel any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged a cent — you keep the free first report regardless.

How to cancel (any time, one place)

You manage everything through Stripe, our payment provider — no need to wait on us:

  1. Open the secure billing portal using the button below.
  2. Enter the email you signed up with. Stripe emails you a one-time login code.
  3. Enter the code to log in — you'll see your subscription.
  4. Click Cancel subscription. Done.
Open the billing portal →
If you cancel after your trial, your subscription stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for — you won't be billed again, and you keep access until then.

Refunds & questions

Charged by mistake, or something's not right? Just email us at admin@webstarweb.com — a real person reads it and we'll sort it out quickly. We're easy to deal with.
